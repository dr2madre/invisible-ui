A **notification center**: a bell with an unread badge that opens a panel of
past notifications. It is the persistent, history side of feedback — the
counterpart to the ephemeral
[Notification](/invisible-ui/components/feedback/notification/) toast. Toasts
come and go; the center is where they stay to be reviewed.

This is a **pattern, not a component** — pure composition of pieces you already
have:

- **[Button](/invisible-ui/components/forms/button/)** (ghost, icon) + **[Count](/invisible-ui/components/data-layout/count/)** — the bell and its unread badge.
- **[Sheet Dialog](/invisible-ui/components/feedback/dialog/sheet-dialog/)** (`side="right"`) — the panel. A lateral sheet, not a popover: a history list is substantial content that wants room, scroll and a footer, and the sheet already brings focus trap, Escape / backdrop dismissal and scroll lock.
- A list of **[Inline Notification](/invisible-ui/components/feedback/inline-notification/)** — one per entry, with the timestamp in the body and a close button to dismiss.

### What the pattern fixes, what the app owns

- **Fixed by the pieces**: the dialog semantics and keyboard/focus behaviour
  (Sheet Dialog), the badge and its accessible name (Count), each row's live
  region, status icon and dismiss affordance (Inline Notification).
- **Owned by the application**: the notification list and its persistence,
  what "unread" means and when it flips, grouping or pagination, and where the
  data comes from. The design system deliberately does not decide these.

### Accessibility

- The bell is a normal button; the unread `Count` carries an accessible name
  (`"3 unread notifications"`), so the badge is not conveyed by color/number
  alone.
- The panel is the Sheet Dialog's modal `role="dialog"` — focus moves in, is
  trapped, and returns to the bell on close.
- Unread rows are distinguished by a surface tint **and** their position/label,
  never by color alone.

### Sketch

```svelte
<SheetDialog side="right" title="Notifications" triggerVariant="ghost">
  <span slot="trigger">
    <BellIcon />
    {#if unread}<Count count={unread} label={`${unread} unread`} />{/if}
  </span>

  {#each items as item (item.id)}
    <InlineNotification status={item.status} title={item.title} closable
      onclose={() => dismiss(item.id)}>
      {item.text}
    </InlineNotification>
  {/each}

  <svelte:fragment slot="footer">
    <Button variant="ghost" onpress={markAllRead}>Mark all read</Button>
  </svelte:fragment>
</SheetDialog>
```
