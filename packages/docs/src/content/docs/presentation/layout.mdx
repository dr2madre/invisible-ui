---
title: "Layout"
---

An **optional presentation layer** — empty for now, by design.

Layout is where page-composition components will live: pieces that arrange
content rather than carry behavior. They sit on top of the system as an
opt-in layer; the primitives underneath stay headless, and nothing in the
other sections will ever depend on this one.

Candidates discussed so far:

- A **non-modal contextual panel** (master–detail, preview pane): content that
  could be its own page, shown alongside the current one. Unlike
  [Sheet Dialog](/invisible-ui/components/feedback/dialog/sheet-dialog/) it does
  not interrupt — the page stays interactive.

Nothing ships here until a real use case asks for it.
